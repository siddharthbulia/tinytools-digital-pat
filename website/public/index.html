Digital Pat

Your friends live on your desktop 🐾

Add a friend with a one-tap invite link and their character hops onto your desktop — and yours onto theirs. Their pet quietly shifts in real time as they code, vibe, or slip into a meeting, so you always have a little sense of what your people are up to. Change your character or mood and it updates on their screen instantly. Works great with just two of you — or your whole crew.

She notices what you're up to

Pat sees which app is in front — and, in your browser, which site you're on — then slips into a matching little mood. She reads only the app and the site name, never the page or what you type.

A little creature, not a widget

Pat lives in your workspace and does her own cute thing — you can always pick her up and put her wherever you like.

🪄

Reacts to your apps

Switch apps and she perks up and changes her little outfit to match the vibe.

💗

Pat her, she purrs

Give her a click — she does a happy slow-blink and floating hearts drift up.

🤏

Drag her anywhere

Grab her and she glides smoothly with your cursor. Drop her wherever feels right.

🐾

Stays out of your way

By default she sits tidily in the top-right and just follows your cursor with a little lean. Want more? Turn on roaming and she'll hop around and peek from the edges.

😴

Naps when you're away

Step away for a bit and she curls up in a corner for a cozy little snooze.

🔒

Stays on your Mac

All local, zero network — nothing is ever sent anywhere. In the browser she peeks at just the site name (the domain) to match your vibe; never the page or your typing.

No tracker. No nags. No account.
Digital Pat is just a sweet companion for your screen — she only ever cheers you on. She stays tucked in the top-right by default; hide her anytime, or let her roam if you want her to wander.
